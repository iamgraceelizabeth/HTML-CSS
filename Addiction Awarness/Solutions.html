  3. Solutions
  • We as a society need to be more open about mental health, because a factor that drives a lot of addictions is stress and the need to find a way to cope with that stress. We need to make mental health resources more available if they aren't already.
  • We need to change the places where there is a high number of these problems. We're talking about changing the social aspects like neighborhood drug use or family dynamics that could support drug use.
  • Government and schools need to go over more addictions than just drugs and alcohol.
